Our Story

We started Doulab to answer a simple yet difficult to answer question: why are entrepreneurship and innovation so hard to do consistently—and how can we make a practical recipe for repeatable, sustainable, and scalable innovation in any context?

That quest gave birth to the MicroCanvas® Framework (MCF), a modular set of canvases that guide teams end‑to‑end, and the Innovation Maturity Model (IMM)®, a structured way to measure and improve innovation capability. Today, we’re expanding that vision with Vigía Futura.

2018

Origins at INFORMATIK

Initial experiments in structured innovation mentoring and coaching; early prototypes of what became MicroCanvas® during Singularity University’s 2018 Incubator and 2019 Global Startup Program (GSP).

During this time we built the first canvas drafts and tested them with 47 early‑stage startups in Silicon Valley during 2018's Singularity University Fall Incubator.

This time was exciting. We started to see initial evidence our approach was effective and more effective than other innovation models.

FAQ

What is the MicroCanvas® Framework (MCF)?

MCF is a modular, easy‑to‑use set of canvases that guide teams through discovery, design, and delivery so innovation becomes repeatable.

What is the Innovation Maturity Model (IMM)®?

IMM is a structured way to assess and improve an organization’s innovation capability across strategy, culture, process, and results.

How does an engagement with Doulab start?

Typically with a quick diagnostic or discovery call. We map goals and constraints, then suggest a path—workshop, program, or coaching.
